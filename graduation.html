DANISH GRADUATION PARTIES
❮ ❯

Danish Graduation parties are total events where we can take care of everything from the premises, doormen, DJ and security for our guests.

We collaborate with a lot of premises all over Zealand so everything is possible. We can also provide a bar within Denmark's alcohol policy. There it will usually be DKK 10-20. bar. Free bar is also an option, send an email and we will look into it.

Price per student for complete event without bar:

80-100 students: 140kr. per student 100-150 students: SEK 120. Per student 150+ students: 100kr. per student

Additional Payment: Party Photographer (100 high quality and edited pictures) - 1500kr

We can also help with booking artists etc. Just email or give us a call beforehand.
